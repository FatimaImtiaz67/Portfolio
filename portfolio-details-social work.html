Social Works

Community Work

Actively involved in various social work and community service initiatives. Believe in using my time and skills to contribute positively to society beyond academics and tech.

I supported a widowed woman who was the sole breadwinner of her family after her husband's passing. She had a serious accident at home that left her with multiple injuries, and I provided her with financial assistance during her recovery to help her and her family through that difficult time.

I prepared and distributed care kits for children in a village community, aiming to bring them small moments of joy. Each kit included basic snacks and treats, thoughtfully packed to brighten their day and share a gesture of kindness with those who have limited access to such comforts.

I volunteered with the Shaukat Khanum Tiger Club , actively participating in donation drives to support cancer patients. As part of the initiative, I engaged with the community to raise awareness and encourage contributions toward the hospital’s mission of providing free cancer treatment to those in need.
